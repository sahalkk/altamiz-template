Description Of The Service

Al Tameez Al Thaqib Contracting Company specializes in comprehensive civil works and construction services across Saudi Arabia. We have been delivering excellence in construction, maintenance, and infrastructure development since 2015, working as a principal contractor on diverse projects ranging from infrastructure to institutional facilities.

Our team consists of professionally qualified and experienced Project Managers, Estimators, Quantity Surveyors, and Site Managers – enabling us to provide an end-to-end service from design through pre-construction, to delivery and aftercare. Supported by carefully selected, pre-qualified subcontractors and suppliers.

Our Services Include

Al Tameez Al Thaqib offers comprehensive civil work services including construction and maintenance of roads, infrastructure, tunnels and bridges, residential and non-residential buildings such as schools, hospitals, airports, hotels, stadiums and railway stations. We also specialize in water and sewage pipelines, electrical power plants including new networks, stations and transformers, dams and construction of onshore pipelines, site preparation and fencing work.
