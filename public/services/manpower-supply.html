Description Of The Service

Al Tameez Al Thaqib Contracting Company provides comprehensive manpower supply services for construction and industrial projects across Saudi Arabia. We have built a trusted network of skilled professionals including construction workers, engineers, technicians, supervisors, and specialized labor to support our clients' workforce needs with excellence and reliability.

Our manpower supply services are managed by experienced human resources professionals who understand the critical importance of having the right people for each project phase. We maintain a diverse pool of qualified workers with expertise in various construction disciplines, from general laborers to highly skilled craftsmen, all properly certified and experienced to meet the demands of modern construction projects.
